Budget tool

This tool is a result of my effort to create a system that make the precification of our services easier, faster and, more importantly, consistent. It was primarily think as tool for internal user, since the one-to-one precification creates an opportunity for third degree price discrimination that we should explore as the life span of this business is necessary limited.

It's important to note that our precification will be based on the cost of production determined by the technology of our more likely competitor. In other words, our price is not a reflection of how hard is for us to complete some task but rather it is a reflection of how hard is to complete a task for the average person. That's why there is a heavy price associated with LaTex formatting, for example. Althought create LaTex document will be standard procedure made by our software, the average person will have a hell of work to create a good TEX document manually.

Last but not least, here is the formula used to create this budget app: $$ P_{i} = [(\alpha_{i} + grammar + form\_gen)\cdot n]\cdot (1+L)\cdot (1+R)$$ Where:

i: is the kind of input document.

\(\alpha_{i}\): constant associated with the kind of document.

\(grammar\): cost of grammar correction per page.

\(form\_gen\): cost of general formatting per page.

L, R: mark-up of LaTex document and reference style, respectively.

The baseline case is the standard formatting of a monograph with MS Word as output document and ABNT as the references style. In this special case the parameters \(\alpha_{i}\), L, and R are set to zero. Otherwise, \(\alpha_{i}\) can be 2 (dissertation), 4 (thesis), and 6 (paper). The LaTex mark-up is set to 20%, while references styles different from ABNT makes the service 5% more expensive. The cost of grammar correction is always 5 reais per page. The page value can also increase (R$ 1,00) if the option for general formatting is chosen.
